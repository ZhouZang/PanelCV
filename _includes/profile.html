My graduate dissertation focuses on the impact of PM2.5 on infectious diseases in China. On working with this dissertation, I realized how important an accurate PM2.5 dataset was and decided to further study the remote sensing of atmospheric aerosols.

Now my work is mainly about using deep learning methods to retrieve aerosols and near surface pollutants at global or national scale, to improve both accuracy and interpretability in satellite retrievals.

My Services 1
Atmospheric Remote Sensing

The satellite-based retrievals of near surface pollutants (PM2.5, O3) and aerosol components (fine mode fraction, fine/coarse mode aerosols).

Deep Learning

Improving the accuracy and interpretability of deep learning models for satellite-based aerosol retrievals.

Spatial Analysis

The long-term, large scale spatiotemporal characteristics of aerosols and near surface pollutants.
